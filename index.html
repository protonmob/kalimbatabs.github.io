Description

The 'Kalimba Lessons: Learn & Play' app, available in the App Store, offers exciting features for kalimba enthusiasts. This application provides easy access to the notes of current and popular songs from a broad music repertoire. Follow step-by-step through videos to enhance your kalimba playing skills. With the premium version, gain unlimited access to an extensive array of song notes, enriching your musical journey. It's an ideal resource for anyone looking to learn and master the kalimba.
